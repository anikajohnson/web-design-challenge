Project Summary

For this project, I looked at how weather patterns vary based on latitude. Below are links to visualizations comparing latitude with weather conditions like cloudiness, humidity, temperature and windspeed.

Data was gathered for this project by generating a list of city weather data from openweathermap.org's Weather API.
